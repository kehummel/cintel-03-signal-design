## Custom Project

### Dataset
The data set tracked different health habits along with an initial weight and an ending weight.

### Signals
-height to weight ratio
-change in weight
-number of calories per step taken

### Experiments
-I looked at the data to find general trends in the people on the list. I looked to see if they lost weight to see if that was the goal. I looked to see if height versus weight was a constant or varied between participants, and then I looked to see if number of steps taken per calorie consumed was consistent between participants.

### Results
-height:weight ratio varied between 1 and 2
-most people lost weight but it was less than one kg
-steps per calorie consumed ranged from 2 to 13

### Interpretation
-The height/weight ratio varied greatly. The data could use some drilling down to see if taller participants or shorter participates had any trends in their ratios.
-There was not a lot of weight loss which would suggest that the time between the initial and current weight was not that long, or that the purpose of the data collection was not about weight loss.
-Again, steps per calorie could use more of a drill down into the data.  It should be looked at whether or not the participants that had more steps per calorie was due to walking more or eating less.
